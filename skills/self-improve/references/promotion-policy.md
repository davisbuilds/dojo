# Promotion Policy

Promotion should be explicit, reviewable, and proportionate to the strength of the evidence.

## Promotion Tiers

- `discard` - weak, stale, duplicated, or non-generalizable signal.
- `keep-local` - worth retaining in the learning store, but not worth durable behavior change.
- `promote-memory-note` - stable lesson worth keeping as a durable operating note in a harness-specific memory surface.
- `promote-skill-candidate` - repeated, generalizable pattern that deserves a draft reusable skill.

## Promotion Checklist

Promote only when most of these are true:

- The pattern recurred more than once or clearly generalizes.
- The change would likely save future work, not just this session.
- The blast radius is understood.
- Verification steps exist and are cheap enough to rerun.
- The proposal artifact is clear enough for another agent or human reviewer to evaluate quickly.

## Review Guidance

- Prefer `keep-local` by default.
- Escalate to `promote-memory-note` when the lesson is stable but still harness-local.
- Escalate to `promote-skill-candidate` only when the pattern is portable and reusable beyond a single project or session.
- Do not skip directly from raw records to live skill edits.
